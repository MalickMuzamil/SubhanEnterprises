Privacy Policy

At Subhan Enterprises, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard customer data related to ATV Quad Bikes, trail bikes, and delivery services across Pakistan.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only essential customer information required to process orders and deliveries. This may include your name, phone number, delivery address, and product selection details. We do not collect unnecessary personal or sensitive data.

2. Payment & Invoicing

Customer payments are accepted for confirmed ATV Quad Bike or trail bike orders. A proper purchase invoice is provided with every delivery. Payment information is used solely for order processing and record keeping.

3. Order Delivery

Subhan Enterprises offers delivery services all over Pakistan. Customer contact details are used only to coordinate vehicle delivery and ensure smooth handover of the ATV.

4. Use of Customer Information

Customer information is used strictly for order confirmation, delivery coordination, mechanical support, and warranty communication. We may request customer feedback or reviews after successful delivery, which is completely optional.

5. Mechanical Support & Parts

Customer information may be used to coordinate mechanical assistance or spare parts support when required. In case of any technical issue, customers are requested to bring the ATV to our designated workshop where proper inspection and repair can be carried out. If any missing or faulty part is identified, the issue will be resolved accordingly at our workshop.

6. Data Protection & Security

All customer information is stored securely and accessed only by authorized staff. We do not sell, rent, or share customer data with third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. Updated versions will be published on this page.

8. Contact Information

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal information, you may contact Subhan Enterprises through our official communication channels.

9. Customer Communication

We may contact customers via phone or messaging platforms only for order confirmation, delivery coordination, warranty communication, or mechanical support purposes. We do not send unnecessary promotional messages.

10. Feedback & Reviews

After successful delivery, customers may be requested to share feedback or reviews regarding their ATV Quad Bike experience. Providing feedback is completely voluntary and helps us improve our services.

11. Third-Party Sharing

Customer information is not shared with third parties except where required to complete delivery or comply with legal obligations. We do not share customer data for advertising or resale purposes.

12. Data Retention

Customer information is retained only as long as necessary for order records, warranty reference, or after-sales support, and is not stored indefinitely.

13. Customer Consent

By placing an order or contacting Subhan Enterprises, customers consent to the collection and use of their information as described in this Privacy Policy.
