Warranty Policy

Subhan Enterprises provides a limited checking warranty on ATV Quad Bikes. This policy covers initial ATV engine and gearbox performance to ensure customer satisfaction at the time of delivery.

1. Warranty Coverage

All new ATV Quad Bikes sold by Subhan Enterprises come with a limited 10-day checking warranty applicable to ATV engine and ATV gearbox components only. This warranty is strictly limited to internal mechanical defects present at the time of purchase.

2. Warranty Period

The warranty is valid for 10 days from the date of delivery. This period is provided solely for inspection and testing of engine and gearbox performance.

3. Included in Warranty
  • ATV engine internal mechanical defects
  • ATV gearbox and gear shifting mechanism issues
4. Not Covered
  • Engine smoke, blow, overheating, or damage due to misuse
  • Issues caused after delivery due to improper running or maintenance
  • Electrical parts, wiring, or accessories
  • Normal wear and tear items
  • Accidents, off-road abuse, or racing damage
5. Assembly Disclaimer

All ATVs are delivered in assembled condition. Subhan Enterprises is not responsible for issues arising due to disassembly, modification, or adjustments performed after delivery.

6. Warranty Claim

Any warranty-related concern must be reported within the 10-day checking period along with the original purchase invoice. All inspections will be conducted by Subhan Enterprises technical staff only.

7. Our Commitment

Subhan Enterprises believes in transparent dealing and customer trust. Every ATV Quad Bike is checked before delivery to ensure proper engine and gearbox functionality.
