Warranty Policy

At Subhan Enterprises, we take pride in delivering reliable and durable ATV Quad Bikes. Our warranty ensures peace of mind, offering protection against manufacturing defects and quality issues.

1. Warranty Policy

Every new ATV purchased from Subhan Enterprises comes with a limited warranty that covers manufacturing defects in the engine, gearbox, and key electrical components under normal use.

2. Warranty Period

The warranty is valid for 6 months from the date of purchase. For heavy-duty or rental use, the coverage may vary as mentioned on your invoice.

3. Included in Warranty
  • Engine and transmission system defects
  • Frame or chassis welding faults
  • Factory-related electrical malfunctions
  • Defective materials or workmanship under normal use
4. Not Covered
  • Accidents, misuse, or racing damage
  • Parts subject to normal wear such as tires, brake pads, and filters
  • Water or rust damage due to improper maintenance
  • Alterations or repairs performed by unauthorized workshops
5. Claim Procedure

To make a warranty claim, customers must provide a valid purchase invoice and contact our authorized service center. Our technical team will inspect the issue and replace or repair the defective part at no cost if eligible.

6. Authorized Service Support

Warranty repairs must be carried out only at authorized Subhan Enterprises service centers. Services performed elsewhere may void the warranty coverage.

7. Customer Responsibilities

Routine maintenance like oil changes, tuning, and cleaning must be performed regularly. Neglecting these may affect vehicle performance and void the warranty.

8. Extended Warranty

Customers can avail Extended Warranty Packages at the time of purchase for up to 12 months of additional protection. For details, contact our dealership team.

9. Our Commitment

Subhan Enterprises is committed to providing top-quality products and customer satisfaction. Each ATV undergoes thorough inspection and testing before delivery to ensure reliability and performance on every ride.
